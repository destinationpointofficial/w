About Us

Shaping Bright Futures
Since Day One

Destination Point is a premier coaching institute dedicated to empowering students from Class 6 to Class 12 under the West Bengal Board curriculum. We believe every student has the potential to excel — our role is to guide them to that destination.

With a team of experienced and passionate educators, we provide comprehensive coaching in all subjects — from Mathematics and Science to English, Bengali, History, Geography and more. Our personalized approach ensures no student is left behind.
